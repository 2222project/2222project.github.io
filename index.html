End poverty by 2222

What We Do

We are attempting a completely new approach to ending poverty, by creating an endowment that will grow very quickly for a very long period of time. Every year we will distribute a small percentage of assets, and give it directly to people in low-income neighborhoods, no strings attached. As the endowment grows, more and more people will benefit.

We won't see the completion of this project, but there's a good chance our great grandchildren will look back on this as the moment when something wonderful was created.

You Can Help

If you give just $10 today, by the year 2222 we expect it will have grown enough to fully support one person for their entire life.

We will begin accepting donations in late summer of 2016, once our nonprofit registration is complete. Please check back again later, or follow us on twitter for updates.

Where We Operate


We are initially focusing on the twenty largest US metropolitan areas. With a combined population of over 110 million people, these regions account for one third of the people the US.
