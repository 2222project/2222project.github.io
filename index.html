End poverty by 2222

What We Do

We are attempting something that has never been done before: end poverty once and for all. We won't see the completion of this project, but there's a good chance our great grandchildren will look back on this as the moment when something wonderful was created.

Our approach is to create an endowment and grow it very quickly, for a very long period of time. Every year we will distribute a small percentage of assets, and give it directly to people in low-income neighborhoods, no strings attached.

As the endowment grows, more and more people will benefit. If you contribute ten dollars today, by the year 2222 we expect it will have grown enough to support one person with over $12,000 per year for their entire life.
