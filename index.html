End poverty by 2222

What We Do

We are creating an endowment to give cash directly to people who need it, with no strings attached. Donors can dedicate contributions to their particular community.

To meet our goal of eliminating poverty by the year 2222, we only need to start with $1 per person.

How It Works

Our approach works because it's based on exponential growth and cash transfers.

Exponential growth. All our contributions go to fund an endowment which targets 5% annual growth after inflation, which means impact is expected to double every 14 years or so. We expect that a donation of $10 today will enable us to give $12,000 per year by 2222.

Cash transfers. Rigorous studies have proven that direct cash transfers are effective [1, 2, 3], because recipients are able to use them to buy whatever they most need. And cash transfers require very little staff to administer relative to the value provided, which allows the vast majority of funds to go directly to the people who need it.

Where We Focus


We are initially focusing on the twenty largest US metropolitan areas. With a combined population of over 110 million people, these regions account for one third of the people in the US.
