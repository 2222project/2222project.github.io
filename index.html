What We Do

We give money directly to people who need it, with no strings attached. We give our recipients the freedom and resources to plan their own life, and we give our donors a way to put their money to work against inequality and racism.

Our endowment enables donors to magnify their impact and sustain that impact for generations to come.

Who We Are

The 2222 Project is a nonprofit organization founded in 2016 by Kim Burchett. She is currently putting together a strong board of directors to guide the project.

We expect to begin accepting donations in the fall of 2016. Please follow us on twitter for updates. In the meantime, if you'd like to volunteer to help us get started, we'd love to have you.
